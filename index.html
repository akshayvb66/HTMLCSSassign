Established in the year 1997, Kendriya Vidyalaya Vijayapura is situated in the historical city of Vijayapura. The Vidyalaya is a CIVIL SECTOR School sponsored by the Government of Karnataka. The Vidyalaya has classes from I to Class XII for the academic year 2018-19. The Vidyalaya has a pupils strength of 967 and staff strength of 39. The Vidyalaya has a spacious campus with a permanent building and all the infrastructural facilities. The Vidyalaya has produced 100% results in Class X & in Class XII CBSE examinations thus mirroring the very essence of teaching and learning process. Under the able guidance of Principal Shri.Siby Sebastian and a staff with unprecedented level of commitment, the school is marching ahead towards excellence.

facilities

The Vidyalaya has a magnificent permanent structure with adequate class rooms, activity hall, art room, music room, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Maths & computer labs, library, Work experience room, Medical room etc. Coolers & Aqua guards are installed for providing safe & pure drinking water to all the students. Computer Education The Vidyalaya is equipped with sophisticated Computer Laboratory with Internet facility and the students from class III onwards are trained by qualified staff. The latest optional subject Computer Science has been introduced at Class XI from the academic year 2006-07 Intel has trained 16 teachers in the computer Application. Science Education To ensure that “Learning by doing” the school has established laboratory for teaching of Physics, Chemistry, Biology Senior section and junior Section lab for a secondary Classes. Science Education is not limited only to the prescribed limited Curriculum. Attitude developed among the students through the guest lectures, Study tours, Science Olympiads etc.
